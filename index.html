what makes us diffrent?

Diwali, also known as Deepavali, is one of the most significant and widely celebrated festivals in India and among Indian communities around the world.

The word "Diwali" translates to a "row of lights," and the festival is aptly named for the breathtaking display of lamps and candles that adorn homes, temples, and public spaces during this time.
